About Thrift & Trend Online

Thrift & Trend Online is a marketplace for affordable lifestyle products — connecting sellers and buyers of thrift wears, shoes, handbags, perfumes, and accessories. The platform promotes sustainable fashion and accessibility, ensuring that quality fashion items are within everyone’s reach.

Our Mission

To create an online marketplace that makes affordable fashion accessible, while promoting sustainability and empowering local sellers.

Our Vision

To become the leading affordable fashion hub in Nigeria, redefining thrift shopping into a modern, trendy, and trusted experience.
